Dynamic Vinyasa

Book This Class

About This Class

Dynamic Vinyasa is a fast-paced, fluid practice that links movement to breath. Designed for those who want to build heat, strength, and endurance, this class will guide you through a series of continuous postures (asanas). The sequence varies by instructor but typically includes sun salutations, standing poses, balancing postures, and a cool-down.

Expect to sweat and challenge yourself mentally and physically. Modifications are offered, but some prior yoga experience is recommended.

What to Bring

  • Yoga Mat (Rentals available)
  • Water bottle
  • Towel
  • Comfortable, breathable clothing
